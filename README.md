# CITY COMPANION
# Overview
CityCompanion is a smart city assistant designed to improve urban living through safety, guidance, and community engagement.
It helps users access essential services while also promoting a safer and more connected environment.

# Problem
~ People moving to a new city often face:
Difficulty finding hospitals, transport, and police stations
Lack of awareness about city rules
Safety concerns and emergency situations
Feeling isolated without community support
Risk of spam or unsafe interactions

# Solution
CityCompanion provides:
A smart assistant for city information
A security-focused system for safer interactions
A community feature to connect people responsibly

# Features
~ Security System
Prevents misuse and unsafe interactions
Focus on user safety and awareness
Designed to reduce spam and harmful usage

~ Smart Assistant
View hospitals with address, contact & map link
Police station details
Emergency helplines
Bus routes between locations
Transport information
City rules
FAQs for common queries
Community & safety-focused concept

~ Community Feature
Connects users with:
Students
Job seekers
Roommates
Encourages safe and meaningful connections

# Tech Stack
